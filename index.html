TIME CAPSULE | an audiovisual ode to my younger selves

when U spoke us into existence, y(our) mouth filled with the taste of saltwater and stardust.

y(our) name dissolves into dark matter.

windswept and widemouthed, U hold flowers, U run free

born in the days approaching winter, U are unflinching under the hand of decay

U can find play in evading capture even when remaining still

an auditory portal into y(our) lofty, liveslong multitudes
